Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

World Cup 2026 Live ("the App") is developed and operated by Appofun. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Favorite Teams: Stored locally on your device only. Never transmitted to our servers.
  • Notification Preferences: Stored locally on your device.
  • App Settings: Theme, language preferences — stored locally on your device.
  • Prediction Game Data: Stored locally on your device only.

2. Information Collected by Third Parties

Google AdMob

The free version of the App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / GAID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage data for ad targeting
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Google Settings → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization). View Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Yandex Advertising Network (selected regions)

In some regions, the App may use Yandex Advertising Network to display advertisements. Yandex may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad targeting. View Yandex's privacy policy at yandex.com/legal/confidential.

Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns and Firebase Crashlytics to detect and fix crashes. These services collect:

  • Anonymous usage statistics (screens viewed, features used)
  • Crash reports (device model, OS version, app version)
  • No personally identifiable information is collected

View Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Google Play Billing

If you purchase "Goals Pro", the transaction is processed by Google Play. We do not store your payment information. Google's privacy policy applies to all purchase transactions.

3. GDPR — Rights of EU/EEA Users

If you are located in the European Union (EU) or European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: contact@microkode.com

We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Consent for Personalized Advertising (EU/EEA)

When you first launch the App in the EU/EEA, we will ask for your consent to show personalized advertisements via a consent dialog. You can change your preference at any time by clearing the App data or reinstalling it.

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent: Personalized advertising (you can withdraw consent)
  • Legitimate Interest: Anonymous crash reporting and app analytics
  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the App functionality

4. Match Data

Live scores and match data are fetched from publicly available sports APIs. No personal information is transmitted when retrieving match data.

5. Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, the App sends local push notifications for match events. Notification preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not use any third-party push notification service that would transmit personal data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device is retained until you uninstall the App or clear the App's data. Firebase Analytics data is retained for 14 months per Google's default policy.

8. International Data Transfers

Data collected by Google (AdMob, Firebase) and Yandex may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States. Google and Yandex implement appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses) for such transfers.

9. Data Security

All user preferences are stored locally on your device using Android's secure SharedPreferences. We do not maintain any servers that store personal user data.

10. Your Rights (Global)

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no data to delete or export from our side. To remove all App data, simply uninstall the App or clear its data from your device settings. For data collected by Google or Yandex, please refer to their respective privacy policies and opt-out tools.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance. EU/EEA users will be re-asked for consent if the purpose of data processing changes significantly.

12. Contact Us & Data Controller

For privacy questions, GDPR requests, or to exercise your rights, contact us at:

contact@microkode.com

We aim to respond within 30 days of receiving your request.
